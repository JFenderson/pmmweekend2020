The 1st PMM Weekend

The first PMM Weekend was on April 12-14, 2019. We had a great turn out for the first year having over 150 people in attendance throughout the weekend.

We paid tribute to the members of the Purple Marching Machine that passed away throughout the years at the picnic. We also paid tribute to the Band Directors(Arthur Means Jr., Donald Crawford, James Merriweather, and Willie Snipes Jr.) of the Purple Marching Machine.

We are excited to put on the 2nd Annual PMM Weekend and we're hopeful for many more.

Enjoy the images from this exciting weekend!
