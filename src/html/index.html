About PMM Weekend

The PMM Weekend is a weekend of events showcasing the family portion of the long standing Miles College Purple Maching Machine. The main purpose of this event is build a funraising hub to donate back to our band and to create a tradition that bridges the gaps between old-school band members, those that marched under the direction of Mr. Arthur Means from 1996-2006, Mr. Donald Crawford from 2007-2012, Mr.James Merriweather from 2012-2015, and the current members of the Purple Marching Machine, under the direction of Mr. Willie Snipes Jr. Jr.
